This project focuses on analyzing restaurant data from Zomato, a popular online food delivery and restaurant discovery platform.
The main goal is to understand customer preferences, restaurant trends, and factors that affect restaurant ratings and popularity.

Using Python and libraries like Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, we clean, process, and visualize the data to gain meaningful insights.

ðŸ”¹ Objectives of the Project

To clean and preprocess Zomato dataset for analysis.

To explore relationships between restaurant features like price range, online delivery, and ratings.

To identify the most popular cuisines and locations.

To visualize important business insights using charts and graphs.

To understand customer preferences and business trends that can help restaurants improve performance.
